Day 2 - Trial

Top

- Overhead Map added to Court Record.

Witness Testimony - The Arrest of Edgeworth - Cross-Examination

- Press Gumshoe on the statement "But the next morning, a body was found in the lake." (Pistol Bullet added to Court Record)
- Press Gumshoe on the statement "So we had to arrest Edgeworth."
- Press Gumshoe on the statement "The murder weapon we found in the boat was decisive evidence." (Pistol added to Court Record)
- Choose "No, I need you here."

Witness Testimony - Witness's Account - Cross Examination.

- Choose "I think there was".
- Press on every statement except the first one, choose "Press further" every time as well.
- Choose "Wrong".

Witness Testimony - That Last Statement - Cross Examination.

- Present Lake Photo to the statement "I saw it as clear as day. The man on the boat was Mr. Edgeworth"

Witness Testimony - How Edgeworth Was Seen - Cross Examination.

- Press Lotta on the statement "Still, I brought my binoculars with me"
- Choose "Press further".
- Present Lotta's Camera to the statement "The camera was set up to take pictures of the meteor shower."
- Choose "Show Evidence", then present Gourdy Article.
- Choose "I have proof", then present Lotta's Camera.

Witness Testimony - Lotta's New Testimony - Cross Examination.

- Present Gourdy Article to the statement "There wasn't much else to look at, so I just watched that boat the whole time."
- Choose "Make her show the enlargement." (Lake Photo added to Court Record)
- Choose "Object to the enlargement".
- Choose the hand that's holding the gun.
- Present Pistol.
- Choose "The victim himself." (Robert's Autopsy Report updated in the Court Record)
- Lotta's Deposition added to Court Record.

Final Day - Trial

Top

Witness Testimony - Why I Left Court - Cross-Examination

- Press Caretaker on the statement "I figured I got nothing to do with this incident anyhow."

- Choose "Yes, I'm doing it."
