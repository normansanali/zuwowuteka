Jana Duggar is a television personality as well as an author. Jana Duggar had come to fame after appearing on the reality shows 19 Kids and Counting and Counting On.

Early Life and Childhood

Jana Duggar was born on 12 January 1990 in Arkansas, USA. As for now, her age is 29 years old and holds an American nationality. She is the daughter of parents named Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar. Similarly, both parents are reality stars.
