Willow And Nina's Tender Reconciliation, Can It Last This Time?

 Posted on July 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  471 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

General Hospital spoilers tease that Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros) may finally have an opportunity to mend fences with her daughter, Willow Tait (Katelyn MacMullen).

There have been many twists and turns in their relationship, so at this point there’s no telling if they can ever bond as mother and daughter.

Willow only discovered that Nina was her biological mother fairly recently. Until that point, she believed that her mother was Harmony (Inga Cadranel). Finding out that wasn’t true was quite a shock for Willow, especially when she discovered her true parentage.

General Hospital Spoilers – Nina Reeves and Willow Tait have a lot of bad blood in their history.

Years before they ever knew they were related; Willow and Nina didn’t get along. But once Nina found out that Willow was hers, she tried everything she could think of to forge a relationship with Willow.

Everything Nina did seemed to pay off when Willow became more open to getting to know her mother. But everything fell apart once Willow discovered that Nina had anonymously tipped off the SEC to Carly Spencer’s (Laura Wright) crime of insider trading. Once again, Willow wanted nothing to do with Nina.

GH Spoilers – Nina Reeves lost Sonny Corinthos!

Nina’s marriage to Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Bernard) imploded once the SEC truth came out. Carly wasn’t the only person implicated in insider trading, as Drew Cain (Cameron Matheson) actually went to prison because of Nina.

Drew’s could only think of revenge when he discovered he went to jail because of Nina.

But during one of their many arguments, Nina and Drew gave in to passion. Shockingly, Drew then agreed to put in a good word with Willow for Nina.

General Hospital Spoilers – Willow Tait may follow Drew Cain’s example!

Once Willow saw Drew and Nina apparently getting along, she began to wonder if she herself hadn’t been hasty in cutting her mother off. She even told Nina that she might be willing to forgive her once again. As happy as Nina is, she will likely wonder how long it will last this time.

Because of their history, Drew can’t exactly be trusted to help Nina long-term. And there’s no telling how Willow would react if she learned of Nina and Drew’s intimate encounters.

But the truth is, Willow and Nina can only go back and forth so many times. Perhaps it’s time for them to permanently embrace their relationship.
