Jason Mesnick, Molly Malaney Share New Picture of Daughter Riley Anne

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  340 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Jason and Molly Mesnick's little girl is already getting so big! The Bachelor couple shared an adorable picture of 4-month-old daughter Riley Anne in a blog post on Monday, Aug. 5.

Jason, 37, and Molly, 28, (nee Malaney) hosted a Country Time Lemonade Stand for the "Great American Lemonade Stand-tacular" campaign to raise money for Alex's Lemonade Stand Foundation, which helps fight childhood cancer. The couple posed for a photo in front of their stand with Molly carrying their beautiful baby girl, who wore a cute yellow dress for the occasion.

The Bachelor stars, who met on season 13 of the ABC reality show in 2009 and married the following year, welcomed Riley on March 14. The Seattle-area dad also has an 8-year-old son, Ty, from a previous marriage. "Ty absolutely loves Riley!" Molly tells Us Weekly. "He cannot get enough of her and no one makes her smile more than Ty! He takes the role of 'big brother' very seriously, and he is so gentle and loving with her."

Molly also tells Us that her baby girl has already "rolled over for the first time last week" and is "non-stop jabbering."

"Having a new baby is one of the hardest jobs in the world, but it has made us love each other so much more," the new mom explains. "We have given each other the greatest gift in the world, with Riley, and for that alone, we have a stronger love than ever before."

While Molly says Jason helps with diaper duty, she's happy to take early morning feedings!

"I literally love everything about motherhood. It doesn't even bother me to wake up at 4 a.m. with a crying baby because that just means I get to spend more time with her," she shares. "I thought for sure that I was be the type of mom that needs 'adult time' and I would have a hard time with running on little sleep, but I am the complete opposite. I honestly cannot get enough of Riley!"
