Suits Cast Congratulates Meghan Markle and Prince Harry on Her Pregnancy

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  371 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry are expecting their first child, and the Duchess of Sussex's former colleagues are beaming with joy for the couple.

The Twitter account for Suits, the drama series in which Meghan starred in for six years, sent their best wishes to the expectant mom on Monday.

"On behalf of the entire #Suits family, we are sending love and congratulations to The Duke and Duchess of Sussex, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, on their pregnancy," the tweet read.

Meghan's costars, including Patrick J. Adams, Gina Torres, Sarah Rafferty, and Rick Hoffman, have not congratulated Meghan over social media just yet, but perhaps they prefer the traditional method of giving Meghan a personal call instead.

However, fans of Meghan and Suits chimed in under the USA Network show's tweet, and joked about how Meghan's on-camera love interest must feel about the news.

"So she went out for milk, married a prince and got pregnant, that’s going to be one huge divorce settlement for you. Get Harvey on it," one fan wrote in jest.

Meghan officially left Suits during her engagement to Prince Harry. A statement posted on Twitter in November 2017 read, "Meghan has been a member of our family for seven years and it has been a joy to work with her. We want to thank her for her undeniable passion and dedication to Suits, and we wish her the very best."

In her first joint-interview with Prince Harry, Meghan shared why she quit acting. "What’s been really exciting, as we talk about this as the transition out of my career... is that the causes that have been very important to me, I can focus even more energy on," she said.

It seems that new transition will now also include motherhood.
