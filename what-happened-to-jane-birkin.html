Jane Birkin Death: What Happened To Jane Birkin?: Born on December 14, 1946, Jane Birkin, officially known as Jane Mallory Birkin was an English singer and actress.

The London-born actress became a household name following her role in the 1966 film, Blow Up, in which she controversially appeared naked in a threesome sex scene.

As a singer, she attained international fame and notability for her decade-long musical and romantic partnership with Serge Gainsbourg, a singer, songwriter, actor, composer, and director.

In 1968, Birkin met Serge Gainsbourg while co-starring with him in Slogan. The duo went ahead to release their debut album Jane Birkin/Serge Gainsbourg in 1969.

Under Gainsbourg’s direction, Birkin also appeared in the controversial film Je t’aime moi non plus and went on to star in Death On The Nile, and Evil Under The Sun.

She also had a prolific career as an actress. Birkin was also a model and came to be widely seen as a fashion trendsetter, inspiring the Birkin handbag, a style put into production by Hermes.
