Sofia Richie Is Reportedly Moving in With Scott Disick

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  290 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Scott Disick and Sofia Richie are reportedly taking the next step in their relationship and moving in together. “She’ll move into his house,” a source told Us Weekly. Disick, 35, and Richie, 19, have been going strong since September 2017.

Getty Images

According to TMZ, Scott moved into a new $3.25 million house in Hidden Hills this April, which is close to his ex-girlfriend and baby mama Kourtney Kardashian. He and the reality star share three children: Mason, 8, Penelope, 5, and Reign, 3.

Sofia has vacationed with the kids in the past, but will likely be spending even more time with the little ones when they come to visit their dad at Disick's home.

Although Scott and Sofia have been dating for a while, Sofia's dad Lionel Richie has not been overwhelmingly supportive of his daughter and her boyfriend, who is 16 years her senior. However, the music icon is letting his daughter decide for herself.

“She’s 19. When you’re 19 you know everything. Is it going to be for life? I don’t know. But for right now it’s just a phase and I’m going to stand real still in the corner, get me a good drink and not make too much noise," Lionel told The Daily Telegraph.

"This is a well-oiled machine, the Kardashian machine," he added. "I don’t really know what to think. I told Sofia I will have no opinion. I’ll continue to be my cheerful self and move on."

Scott and Sofia have been spotted traveling everywhere together, from Italy, to Saint Barts, to Cabo.

Kourtney Kardashian has also moved on from her and Disick's former relationship, and is now dating French model Younes Bendjima. They're currently traveling around Italy together with her kids.
