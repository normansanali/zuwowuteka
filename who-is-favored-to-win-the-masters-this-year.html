Who is favored to win the Masters this year?

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  582 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The upcoming Masters tournament at Augusta National is generating significant buzz and anticipation among golf enthusiasts worldwide.

With a field featuring a diverse range of talented golfers, the competition promises to be captivating.

In this Fan Arch preview, we will take a look into the potential favorites to win the Masters this year, providing detailed insights into their recent performances, past records in the Masters, and other relevant factors that contribute to their chances of winning.

Christo Lamprecht

  • Christo Lamprecht, the current No. 1-ranked amateur golfer in the world, stands out as a notable contender for the low-amateur title at the 2024 Masters.
  • The 6-foot-8 South African, who attends Georgia Tech, has already displayed his potential by being the low-amateur in his lone previous major start.
  • Lamprecht's impressive record and local support, including friends and family in attendance, position him as a strong contender for a standout performance at Augusta National.

Ludvig Aberg

  • Ludvig Aberg, another promising player, is favored to be the top debutant at the Masters with +275 odds.
  • Aberg's remarkable rise from being the 905th ranked player in the world to the 9th rank, along with his consistent top 25 finishes and elite ball-striking abilities, make him a compelling prospect for success at the Masters.

Xander Schauffele

  • Xander Schauffele, with favorable odds to win at +1400 and to finish in the top 10 at +140, is a strong contender for the Masters title.
  • Schauffele's exceptional statistics, including a true strokes gained average of +2.37 over the last 50 rounds, and his consistent performance at Augusta National, with four top-20 finishes in six starts, make him a formidable force in the competition.

Joaquin Niemann

  • Joaquin Niemann, with odds to win at +2800 and to finish in the top 10 at +230, is another golfer to watch out for at the Masters.
  • Niemann's impressive work on the LIV Tour, coupled with his consistent gains in strokes gained approach and off the tee, positions him as a strong contender for a standout performance at Augusta National.

Matt Fitzpatrick

  • Matt Fitzpatrick, with odds to win at +4000 and to finish in the top 10 at +300, is a player with a strong track record at Augusta National.
  • Fitzpatrick's two consecutive top-15 finishes at the Masters, coupled with his consistent gains in strokes off the tee and approach play, make him a player to keep an eye on in the tournament.

Sahith Theegala

  • Sahith Theegala, with odds to win at +5500 and to finish in the top 20 at +160, is another golfer with the potential to make an impact at the Masters.
  • Theegala's exceptional debut at Augusta National last year, along with his consistent performance since then, positions him as a strong contender for a notable finish at the tournament.

Russell Henley

  • Russell Henley, with odds to win at +6500 and to finish in the top 20 at +160, is a player with a recent strong performance at the Valero Texas Open, finishing solo 4th.
  • Henley's consistent success at Augusta National, including a T4 finish last year and T31 or better in six consecutive years, makes him a compelling contender for a noteworthy performance at the Masters.

The 2024 Masters tournament at Augusta National promises to be an enthralling event, with a diverse field of talented golfers vying for victory.

Each of the players mentioned above brings a unique set of skills, recent successes, and past records at Augusta National, making them all strong contenders for a standout performance at the prestigious tournament.
