Madison Nicole Ziegler aka Maddie Ziegler is an American Actress, Dancer, Model, and Author who has a net worth of $5 Million. She is known for portraying Christina Sickleman in the drama film “The Book of Henry” (2017). Initially, she was appeared in the television dance reality show “Dance Moms” (2011 to 2016) at age of 8 years along with her sister Mackenzie Ziegler. She gained huge recognition for featuring in a music video series by Sia such as “Chandelier” and “Elastic Heart”.

She also voiced the character Camille Le Haut in the animated adventure comedy film Ballerina (2016), directed by Éric Summer and Éric Warin.
