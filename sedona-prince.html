Sedona Prince is a college basketball player from America. She plays in Oregan Duck women’s basketball team representing the University of Oregan, wearing a number 32 jersey. She plays in the position of forward with the advantage of her huge height; she is suitable for the position. She was born in Hemet, California, the United States, on May 12, 2000.

Currently, she is playing as a redshirt sophomore showing her remarkable performance. She has to wait a year after being denied by NCAA following her transfer to Texas.

As a national player, she appeared in the different national tournaments as U18, U17, and U16 USA national team. She has also won the 2017 FIBA U17 World Championship in Zaragoza, Spain, and the 2015 FIBA U16 Americas Championship in Mexico.
