Kim and Kanye Have An Early Birthday Bash

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  137 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kim Kardashian and Kanye West are kicking off the rapper's 40th birthday celebrations early with a private getaway to the Bahamas this weekend.

The couple brought their two children North and Saint along with close friends and family for the trip in honor of Kanye's June 8th birthday.

According to E! News, sources revealed, "They rented out a huge luxurious amazing villa... They have had cases of Casamigos flown in and are drinking by their private pool. The kids all have their own play area and they all look like they are having a great time."

The special vacation comes right after the couple's three-year wedding anniversary, which they celebrated last week.

It's been a while since Kim and Kanye have been spotted on vacation together, so here's to hoping for some good family beach Instagrams.
