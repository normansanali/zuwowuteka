Who Is The Richest Actor From The 'Spy Kids' Cast Today?

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  981 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

20 years ago, one of the most iconic films of many people’s childhoods was introduced to our screens as Robert Rodriguez's project changed the standards for modern children’s films. The creator's spy comedy Spy Kids quickly became a huge success upon its release as it became and still continues to be a household favorite for families worldwide.

The film, which would later become the first of a quadrilogy, followed the story of two young kids, Carmen Cortez (Alexa Vega) and Juni Cortez (Daryl Sabara), children of the alluring couple Gregorio (Antonio Banderas) and Ingrid Cortez (Cara Gugino). Whilst the four may have superficially seemed like a regular nuclear family, the reality was far more exciting than their facade would have had you thinking— they were all spies! The film’s success proved immeasurable for the actors whose careers it kickstarted, but just how far did the film franchise take them and who holds the number one spot for richest today?

8 Daryl Sabara

Daryl Sabara portrayed the young, fun-loving Juni Cortez in the franchise. His character often found himself overshadowed by his sister, yet in critical moments he would pull through and prove his spy abilities. By the time the third film of the franchise, Spy Kids: Game Over, was released, Sabara’s character had developed into a fine young spy and even led the feature. Since Spy Kids, Sabara has undertaken some roles in both film and television. Most recently Sabara became a father when his wife Meghan Trainor gave birth to their baby boy Riley Sabara in February 2021. Celebrity Net Worth reports his net worth to come in at $750 thousand.

7 Emily Osment

After her role as the Cortez siblings’ biggest rival, Gerty Giggles, Emily Osment went on to have a pretty successful career. She continued her career in children’s entertainment when she got the role of Lilly Truscott in the hit Disney Channel series, Hannah Montana, in 2006. The show lasted 5 years on air with the final episode airing in 2011. Since then, the actress has also developed a singing career. Osment's net worth reportedly comes in at $3 million.

6 Alan Cumming

Scottish actor Alan Cumming portrayed the role of Fegan Floop, a quirky showrunner with a passion for cloning and bizarre experiments. Outside of the Spy Kids series, Cumming continues to develop a successful creative career showcasing not only his acting skills but also those as a singer and writer. With an impressive array of accolades under his belt such as an Independent Spirit Award and even a couple of Olivier Awards, it’s no doubt that the talented actor possesses a special finesse towards his craft. However with Celebrity Net Worth reporting his net worth at $5 million, Cumming only takes the sixth spot on this list.

5 Alexa PenaVega

Known for her role as the critical thinking, level-headed Carmen Cortez, Alexa PenaVega was thrust into the limelight following the success of Sky Kids. Since then the actress has undertaken several roles in both film and television. PenaVega also ventured into the world of singing as she began to release singles to accompany her acting roles. Her current net worth stands at $8 million.

4 Danny Trejo

Moving onto rough and ready Danny Trejo, the rugged mechanic known as Machete in the Spy Kids series. Prior to his role in Spy Kids, Trejo had lived a long complicated life of crime, incarceration, and redemption. His roller-coaster journey of a career saw him become the staple Hollywood tough guy. Trejo’s net worth comes in at $8 million, making him neck and neck with his co-star PenaVega on the list.

3 Steve Buscemi

Coming in at number three on the list is the talented Steve Buscemi. Buscemi portrayed the anxious Romero— a mad scientist who lives in a constant state of terror of the animal hybrids he created— in the second installment of the film series, Spy Kids: Island Of Lost Dreams. He also made a brief appearance in the third film, Spy Kids: Game Over. Outside the Spy Kids series, Buscemi has worked alongside some of Hollywood’s finest talents such as Quentin Tarantino and Adam Sandler. In 1993 Buscemi portrayed the role of Mr. Pink in Tarantino’s Reservoir Dogs. Buscemi ranks third due to his $35 million net worth.

2 Antonio Banderas

Perhaps one of the most recognizable faces in the entire Spy Kids franchise, it’s no secret that Antonio Banderas had been and continues to be an established and successful actor outside of the children’s series. He is most recognized for his roles in the legendary director Pedro Almodovar’s films. Coming in with a whopping $50 million net worth, this Hollywood legend ranks second on the list.

1 George Clooney

And finally coming in at number one on this list of talented actors and actresses is Hollywood’s "it man" himself, George Clooney. It is only to be expected that the Hollywood powerhouse is at the top of his league with several Academy Awards and countless other honors. His net worth seems to reflect this, as Clooney comes in at a tremendous $500 million! Perhaps best reflected by his lavish lifestyle, it is no surprise that Clooney is crowned first at the very top of this list of actors and actresses. For example, this can be seen in the actor and his wife, Amal Clooney’s, current property portfolio. Although Clooney only portrayed a small role in the Spy Kids franchise, the net worth he has accumulated over the span of his successful career is incomparable to that of his co-stars.
