Who is Jiri Vesely?

Jiri Vesely was born on July 10, 1993 in Příbram, Czech Republic Jiri Vesely and Irena Vesely. He has two siblings; sister Aneta and Natalie with whom he grew up along with. His father is a former tennis player and coach.

Vesely began playing tennis at age 4, after he went to training with his father. He grew idolizing Roger Federer and Tomas Berdych. His favorite shot as a player is serve and favorite surfaces are hard and clay.

In 2009, Vesely turned professional and has been participating in all tournaments.
