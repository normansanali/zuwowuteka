David Lee Murphy was born in Herrin, IL on January 7, 1959. Country singer whose song “Just Once” for the film 8 Seconds reached the Country Billboard charts in 1994. He also released top ten hits like “Loco.” He co-wrote the song “Living in Fast Forward” for Kenny Chesney. He worked behind the scenes on albums for Reba McEntire and Doug Stone .
