Biography

Naomi Neo (born January 25, 1996) is a gifted Singaporean content creator who excels as a blogger, model, and social media personality. She embarked on her journey as a blogger in 2011, eventually evolving into a content creator on YouTube in 2012. With her relatable and entertaining style, she has garnered a dedicated following.

Naomi Neo‘s content portfolio is as diverse as it is captivating, covering an array of subjects ranging from fashion and beauty to lifestyle and travel. What sets Naomi apart is her collaborative spirit, working alongside prominent Singaporean content creators such as Night Owl Cinematics and JianHao Tan. 

Her active presence on social media platforms is a testament to her influence; She boasts over 865k followers on Instagram, over 1.6 million fans on TikTok, and an impressive over 495k subscribers on YouTube.

Early Life & Education

Born on January 25, 1996, in Singapore, Naomi Neo‘s unique family background adds intrigue to her story. Her father was a barber, and her biological father was a teacher. Naomi revealed in a video that she was adopted from China.

Raised in Singapore, Naomi Neo pursued a communications and new media degree at the National University of Singapore. Her educational journey also included Damai Secondary School and the Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts, where she graduated with a diploma in Arts Management in 2016.

Naomi Neo‘s success story exemplifies dedication and authenticity, serving as inspiration, particularly to young women. Despite her achievements, she remains grounded, valuing family and community. Her education at Damai Secondary School and the Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts significantly influenced her career.

Career

Naomi Neo, a versatile YouTuber and social media influencer, is also a successful entrepreneur. She founded NNCHANTED, an accessories company, and nnpower, a holistic wellness brand. Her financial prowess is evident, earning a substantial six-digit annual income from sponsored content. Her YouTube channel boasts over 495,000 subscribers, with recent content focusing on her journey into motherhood.

Beyond the digital realm, Naomi Neo has graced the Asia Style Collection runway alongside K-pop icons and worked as a model for various blogshops. However, she faced criticism and support for a TikTok “ghost prank” on her son, Kyzo.

Naomi Neo‘s life also includes material indulgence, notably a Lamborghini Huracán, a dream acquisition she gifted herself. She previously owned a Mercedes C Class.

In November 2022, Naomi Neo teased her followers with an Instagram post about her new three-story home, complete with a hot tub and pool, a project three years in the making.

Her journey began with a blog and YouTube channel during her university days. Now, Naomi Neo‘s not only a social media influencer but also an advocate for social causes and an actress.

Personal Life

In early 2016, Naomi Neo‘s love story with Han began, culminating in their enchanting wedding at the Singapore Botanic Gardens in March 2018, followed by a private yacht reception at the Marina at Keppel Bay. In the same month, Naomi joyfully revealed her pregnancy in a YouTube video titled “I AM PREGNANT!!!” when she was already six months along. Their son, Kyzo Han, was welcomed into the world in June 2018.

Fast forward to August 22, when Naomi Neo shared a captivating YouTube video titled “How I Hid My Pregnancy From My Husband For 3 Months,” unveiling her second pregnancy. In January 2021, their family joyously expanded with the arrival of their daughter, Zyla Rey.

Before her life with Han, Naomi Neo dated JianHao Tan in 2014, marked by delightful collaborations on YouTube comedy videos. After more than a year of shared adventures, they gracefully parted ways, their bond remaining strong. JianHao Tan even visited Naomi Neo after the birth of her first child in 2018.

Naomi Neo‘s life is a tapestry woven with love, family, and the joyous pages of motherhood, graced by the presence of her cherished children, Kyzo and Zyla Rey.
