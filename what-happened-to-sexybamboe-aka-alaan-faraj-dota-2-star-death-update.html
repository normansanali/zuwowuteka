Unconfirmed reports regarding the death of former Dutch Dota 2 player Sexybamboe, real name Alaan Faraj, are dispersing on Twitter, Reddit, and Discord.

Alaan “SexyBamboe” Faraj was a professional e-sports player. 

Faraj had played for over a dozen teams from various regions and had made a name for himself in the professional Dota 2 scene.

What Happened to Sexybamboe Aka Alaan Faraj? Dota 2 Star Death Update

Rumors say that Dota 2 star Sexybamboe, aka Alaan Faraj, passed away at the age of 29.  

SexyBamboe was seriously depressed, according to a recent discord conversation between Faraje’s brother and a close friend.

Before the death rumors, Alaan had not communicated with his fans or family members in weeks. 

Since the gamer hadn’t streamed in a long time, fans were already concerned about him weeks before the death allegations surfaced.

Sexybamboe Age: How Old Is He? 

Sexybamboe is now 29 years old. He was born on January 20, 1992. 

Alaan Faraj was born and raised in the Netherlands. He had started playing online with a Dutch e-sports team at a young age.

Sexybamboe began playing professionally long before DotA 2 got launched.

He played DotA 1 as a professional for team GoD in 2009 and team DCcom in 2011.   

Sexybamboe Wikipedia Explored  

SexyBamboe began his Dota 2 stint with LowLandLions, a Dutch Dota team that was previously identified as GoD in DotA. 

Sexybamboe’s best roles in the game were Offlaner and Support. However, he mostly played the offlane role for the professional teams.

The professional Dota 2 player had an eight-year career. Since leaving Team Bald, he hadn’t joined another team. 

Among the dozens of teams that he was a part of, the most notable are Evil Geniuses, HellRaisers, and GeekFam.

Faraj competed in The International 2012 with mousesports, where he teamed up with Dota 2 legendary figures KuroKy and SingSing.

He had the highest average death rate per game during the tournament.

Sexybamboe Net Worth: How Rich Is The Former Dota 2 player?  

The estimated net worth of former Dota 2 player Sexybamboe is $100,000. 

According to the data on Liquipedia, the player’s total earnings from competing in the gaming tournament were approximately $85,450.

In addition to competing, the Dota 2 veteran streamed on video streaming sites such as YouTube and Twitch, which definitely earned him some extra money.
