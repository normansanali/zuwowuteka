In the glitzy world of Floyd Mayweather, not everything is as it seems. Known for his opulence and extravagance, Mayweather dropped a peculiar revelation. An Instagram clip offers a glimpse into his time-telling treasures. But, here’s the oddity: amidst a sea of luxury, it’s not the $18 million piece that’s closest to his heart.

As fans tuned in, awaiting a boastful showcase of his priciest possession, Mayweather veered off-script. He spotlighted a recent purchase, stirring curiosity. It’s not just about the heftiest price tag in his collection. Mayweather, always full of surprises, has yet another curveball for us. What watch could possibly overshadow his famed Jacob & Co. ‘Billionaire’?

45 days, 45 watches: Mayweather’s travel essentials

Floyd Mayweather’s watch collection is a dazzling testament to his success outside the boxing ring. Often dubbed the ‘Timepiece Titan,’ Mayweather’s assortment features some of the most exquisite and rare watches in the world. Yet, it’s not just about numbers and brands; it’s a reflection of his journey, tastes, and passions.

Transitioning from his general collection, in a recent Instagram video, Mayweather got candid. He remarked, “So when I travel, I like to take, you know, if I am going for 45 days, I like to take 45 watches with me.” Highlighting his penchant for options, he continued, “Everybody is always asking me ‘What’s your favorite watch?’ Well, one of my favorite watch brands is Avi & Co. As you can see right here,” he said, proudly showcasing his wrist, “I got this a few months back. It’s one of my favorite watches. One of the best brands in the world. That’s just my personal opinion.”

And if that endorsement wasn’t clear enough, he added, “I like the brand so much, I went and bought another watch just yesterday.” His genuine admiration for Avi & Co, amidst a sea of luxurious brands, undoubtedly emphasizes the brand’s unique allure in Mayweather’s eyes.

Floyd Mayweather Unveils Limited Edition Avi & Co. Watches

Boxing icon Floyd Mayweather took to Instagram, offering fans an exclusive peek into his illustrious watch collection. But it wasn’t just any showcase; Mayweather was flaunting the new limited edition Avi & Co. AH10 and AH17 timepieces. With prices ranging from $250,000 to a staggering $370,000, these watches are more than mere accessories; they’re statements.

While Mayweather clarified he’s not in the watch business, his passion for horology was palpable. Addressing his vast fanbase, he exclaimed, “I don’t know about your other Juhu watches, but this by far is the best.” The champ’s bold assertion rings clear: for Mayweather, only the best will do. This endorsement, coming from a man accustomed to luxury, places Avi & Co. watches at the pinnacle of opulence.

As the boxing legend continues to evolve, so does his collection, sparking wonder and admiration from fans and watch enthusiasts alike.
