Official Rules for Us Weekly’s “Show Us Your Family’s Halloween Costumes” Contest

NO PURCHASE REQUIRED TO ENTER OR WIN.

4. How to Enter:

a. Entrants may enter the “Show Us Your Family’s Halloween Costumes” Contest (the “Contest”) via Instagram and/or Twitter. Entrants should follow @UsWeekly on Instagram and/or Twitter, and ensure that the “Photos Are Private” option (“Protect my Tweets” option on Twitter) in the settings on your social account is set to “OFF”. On your public (not protected) account, upload your photographic submission (“Submission”), keeping in mind the five objective judging criteria listed below, and in the caption for the photo/video include custom hashtags #UsDoesHalloween along with reply to @UsWeekly; OR

b. Entrants may also enter the Contest by mailing their name, address, as well as their Submission to: Us Weekly LLC,1290 Avenue of the Americas, New York, New York 10104. Attn: Entry For Us Weekly’s “Show Us Your Family’s Halloween Costume” Contest to be considered. Entries must be postmarked no later than October 20, 2016 and received no later than October 23, 2016.

Limit one entry per person. If more than one (1) entry is submitted, only the first entry received will be judged. Contest begins at 9:00 a.m. Eastern Standard Time (“EST”) on 10/19/2016. All Submissions must be received by 11:59 p.m. EST on 10/23/2016.

For further Submission dates and guidelines, see paragraphs 5, 6 and 8.

For details of Sponsor policies regarding the use of personal information collected in connection with this Contest, visit Sponsor’s privacy policy at http://www.usweekly.com/services/privacypolicy.

5. Judging/Selection of Grand Prize Winner:

a. Submissions will be judged by a qualified panel of judges selected by Sponsor (“Judges”) who will consider the following criteria (“Judges’ Criteria”): cuteness (33.33%), humor (33.33%), and creativity/originality (33.34%). In the event of a tie, the entry with the higher score first in the creativity/originality category, then in the special technique skill category and then in the subject/interest skill category will be declared the Finalist.

The questions below are illustrative of the types of questions and factors judges will take into consideration in assessing the outlined criteria:

Cuteness : Is the photo adorable; lovable; endearing?

Humor : Is the photo amusing; witty; comical?

Originality/Creativity : Does the image depict an original subject and/or approach to a standard subject?

The winners will be notified by a comment from @UsWeekly to the winner’s photo submission on the winner’s Instagram account on or around October 26.

The decisions of the Judges and of Sponsor will be final and binding on all matters relating to the Contest.
