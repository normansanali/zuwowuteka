A$AP Rocky And Kathy Griffin Were Very Flirty In A 2013 Video But Did They Ever Date?

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  855 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Before A$AP Rocky started dating Rihanna and having two kids with her, he was linked to fellow musicians, Iggy Azalea and Rita Ora who's now married to Taika Waititi. He also dated models, Kendall Jenner and Chanel Iman.

In an interview, the Peso rapper said that Iman was the "perfect girl" at the "wrong time" and that he was "so in love" with her. However, he admitted that they were "young and it was something that was so real that it was so serious."

He went on to say that after dating the Victoria's Secret Angel from 2012 to 2014, he was "not okay with losing her but that's the way of the world." He then dated Love & Hip-Hop: New York star Tahiry Jose from 2014 to 2017.

In February 2017, he dated Drake's baby mama, Sophie Brussaux. Somewhere in between, in 2013, Rocky got flirty with canceled comedian Kathy Griffin in a YouTube video, which also had people talking back then. Here's the truth about their relationship.

Is Kathy Griffin Married Now?

Kathy Griffin has been in a relationship with Randy Bick since 2012. They separated in 2018 and reconciled months later in April 2019. They tied the knot in 2020 at the actress' California home. The ceremony was officiated by Grace & Frankie star, Lily Tomlin.

In a clip tweeted by Griffin, Tomlin was filmed saying: "What was supposed to be a shallow, 'toot it and boot it' one-night stand has grown and flourished into something far more meaningful. They stayed together. Then they couldn't stay away from one another."

At that time, the bride also tweeted that her wedding dress was the same outfit she wore on their first date. "I asked Randy to pick out which ever one of my long dresses was his favorite for any reason," she wrote. "He picked out this dress and showed me this photo [of their first date]. Romance is hotttt again."

Bick has been with Griffin throughout her lung cancer battle which she announced to her fans via Instagram in August 2021. "I've got to tell you guys something. I have cancer," she said back then. "I'm about to go into surgery to have half of my left lung removed. Yes, I have lung cancer even though I've never smoked!"

In December 2021, she said in an episode of Jimmy Kimmel Live that she was "officially cancer-free." She also explained that she sounded different due to her operation. "So now I'm like Minnie Mouse meets Marilyn Monroe," Griffin said of her new voice.

Is A$AP Rocky Planning To Marry Rihanna?

There have been rumors about Rocky and Rihanna being engaged or even secretly married. However, neither has confirmed any of the claims.

Ahead of the latter's Super Bowl performance, a source said that the couple had planned on getting married that month but the former Savage X Fenty CEO obviously had a busy schedule.

"She would love to walk down the aisle with a baby bump – and they won't be stopping at two children, either," said the insider. "Being a mum has changed Ri's life for the better in every way. She and Rocky are totally in sync as parents and are loving the experience."

They added that Rihanna's second pregnancy "brought them so much closer as a couple" and that she "wants a big family – she doesn't see any need to hang around and feels ready now her body has fully healed and recovered from the delivery in mid-May."

Talking about the allegedly postponed wedding plans, the source said: "[They] had to put it off a couple of times because they wanted to get through the pregnancy, then settle into raising their little guy for at least six months without any big distractions." (via Capital FM).

Did A$AP Rocky And Kathy Griffin Date?

In an episode of Back & Forth with Noisey, Griffin and Rocky sat down together to basically talk dirty with each other. Right at the beginning, the former tells the hip-hop artist: "I swear to God, I will ---- you because you're all talk, honey."

There was even a part when the A$AP Mob member asked The Cable Guy star: "If you had to pick a female that you like to kiss, who would she be? With me, though, like a trifecta kiss," to which Griffin said, Rihanna.

"This is obvious, like a cliche but I'm gonna have to go Rihanna," she responded. "I am! She's so beautiful, I love her music, she's undeniably gorgeous." Rocky smiled. He then quipped: "You know what, I'm gonna tell Riri that you want to have a trifecta kiss with you, me, and her... Imma hook it up."

Griffin and Rocky weren't a thing. At that time, the latter was in a relationship with Iman. The three were even photographed hanging out together one time.
