Leah Messer's Daughter Throws Tantrum, Gets Spanked on Teen Mom 2

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  208 words  |  Larita Shotwell

More than a handful! Leah Messer’s life is not picture perfect. The Teen Mom 2 star is juggling three rowdy children, a custody battle with her ex-husband, Corey Simms, and relationship problems with her husband, Jeremy Calvert.

In a new sneak peek clip for the July 30 episode, the West Virginia native is at her wit’s end trying to handle her three girls.

“I just feel stressed out now,” Leah tells her friend. “I forgot today’s the first day they’ve been back [from Corey’s house]. Maybe it’s the transition from his house to my house. Ali does good, it’s more Gracie.”

One of Leah and Corey’s twin daughters, Aleeah Grace, acts out, screaming and kicking the toy bicycle her baby sister is riding on.

“Gracie! Get that right now,” Leah demands. “I’m not playing. You’re going to pick that up! You’re not going to act like this in front of people. Do you understand? I’ve had enough. I don’t know why you’re acting like this.”
