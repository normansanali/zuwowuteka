Laura Prepon's Real Hair Color

In 1998, Laura Prepon made her acting debut on the FOX sitcom, 'That 70s Show'. Prepon played the role of Donna Pinciotti and did so for the show's entire 8 season run. While fans fell in love with Laura as Eric Forman's next-door neighbor, a new wave of fans developed after Prepon secured her role as Alex Vause on the Netflix series, 'Orange Is[tldr_position] The New Black'. While her performance in both was outstanding in so many ways, her appearance changed up quite a bit.

During her time on 'That 70s Show', Laura Prepon played a redhead and did so for most of the shows run. In 2004, Laura took a role in a movie called 'Karla', where she sported a blonde look. Considering this film was shot at the same time as her time on FOX, Laura's character, Donna, sported blonde hair in the show, too! From red hair to blonde, to playing a brunette on 'OITNB', fans began to wonder what Laura Prepon's real hair color is.

While she can pull off just about any looks spectacularly, Laura's real hair color is red! The star is naturally a redhead and showed off her stunning locks throughout the entire series. So, if you were ever confused over what her true color is, then worry no more, because it has been red since day 1! Prepon revealed this fact during her 2012 '25 Things You Didn't Know About Me' interview with Us Weekly.

This was easily Prepon's signature look and one that fans think back on quite often considering how popular 'That 70s Show' has become ever since it began streaming on Netflix. Since her success on the show, Prepon has gone on to appear in many shows as a guest star including, 'How I Met Your Mother' and has voiced characters on several animated television shows, including 'American Dad'. Not only does she work hard, but she always looks great doing it!
