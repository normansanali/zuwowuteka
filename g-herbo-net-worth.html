G Herbo Career

G Herbo is a rapper from Chicago, Illinois.

The mixtape received critical acclaim and introduced G Herbo to a wider audience.

In 2015, G Herbo signed a deal with Cinematic Music Group and released his second mixtape, Ballin Like I’m Kobe.

The mixtape featured guest appearances from Lil Durk, Lil Reese, and Sonta.

In 2016, G Herbo released his third mixtape, Strictly 4 My Fans, which served as a prelude to his debut album.

The following year, G Herbo released his debut album, Humble Beast, which debuted at number 21 on the Billboard 200 chart.

The album included collaborations with Lil Uzi Vert, Bump J, and Jeremih.

In 2018, G Herbo released a deluxe edition of Humble Beast, as well as a joint album with Southside, Swervo.

The joint album peaked at number 15 on the Billboard 200 chart and spawned the hit single “Who Run It”.

In 2019, G Herbo released another joint album with Southside, Still Swervin, as well as his second solo album, PTSD.

The latter album addressed G Herbo’s struggles with post-traumatic stress disorder and featured guest appearances from Juice WRLD, Chance the Rapper, Lil Durk, and 21 Savage.

In 2020, G Herbo released his third solo album, 25, which debuted at number 5 on the Billboard 200 chart and became his highest-charting album to date.

The album included collaborations with Polo G, Gunna, Lil Tjay, and Rowdy Rebel.
