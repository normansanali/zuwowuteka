Darby Allin Makes a Bold Statement with a Large Face Tattoo

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  256 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Darby Allin is known for taking chances outside the ring. It turns out that he also sat down in the tattoo artist’s chair recently for some pretty extreme ink.

AEW has Darby Allin’s image and likeness in many promotional campaigns all over the place. He is on t-shirts, action figures, and the company’s video game. Now he has done something to really change up his look.

Darby Allin was spotted backstage with a new face tattoo. The new ink is a pretty large skeleton arm, and it goes right over his forehead.

AEW fans have strong opinions, so odds are there will be plenty of comments over this new tattoo. Darby Allin certainly loves tattoos, and he’s in a company that won’t tell him not to get new tattoos.

In other tattoo news, Solo Sikoa got pretty big piece on his hand. That tribal art on Solo’s hand is really nothing compared to Darby Allin’s new tattoo. Only time will tell how many more tattoos Darby Allin gets at this point, but you never know what he will do next.
