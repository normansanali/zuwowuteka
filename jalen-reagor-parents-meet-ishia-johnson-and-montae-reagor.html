Jalen Reagor Parents: Meet Ishia Johnson And Montae Reagor

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  155 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ishia Johnson and Montae Reagor are the parents of NFL player Jalen Reagor.

Jalen’s mother Ishia Johnson’s details are under wraps but his father Montae Reagor is a former defensive tackle who spent nine seasons in the National Football League (NFL).

He played college football for Texas Tech University, and he was recognized as an All-American.

Montae Reagor attended Waxahachie High School and was a standout high school football player for the Waxahachie Indians.

He was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the second round of the 1999 NFL Draft, and also played for the Broncos, Indianapolis Colts (with whom he won Super Bowl XLI) and Philadelphia Eagles of the NFL. Reagor was a coaching intern for the Eagles in 2011.

The 45-year-old Reagor sold his Super Bowl XLI ring on the famous TV show Pawn Stars.

Ishia Johnson and Montae Reagor’s son Jalen Reagor play in the NFL as a wide receiver for the Minnesota Vikings.
