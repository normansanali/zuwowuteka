Cindy Crawford Style for JCPenney: Bedding

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  294 words  |  Martina Birk

I want my bedroom to be a haven—a place where I can get away from it all. From bedding to those special touches, my new home collection for JCPenney has everything you need to create the perfect retreat.

I like to start with bedding—choose a color palette and design that will set the scene for the rest of the room (I’ve always loved blue and think it’s so easy to decorate with).

I love sheets that are soft to the touch. We all want our bed to look great, but it’s even more important for it to feel great. That’s why my sheets are 400-thread count—they feel so good you can’t wait to get into bed every night.

Once you have the bedding and sheets selected, add the finishing touches. Layering a down-alternative blanket adds texture and instantly enhances your bed style. And stylish pillows create a luxurious statement in seconds—I like to use three Euro pillows on beds for extra cushy luxury. It’s the little touches that complete the look.

More of my favorite style tips:

•    A luggage rack adds that personal touch to make your guests feel right at home.

•    Hang mirrors horizontally or vertically to change up your space and add personality.

•    Adding a bench or storage trunk under a window can create a new and unexpected seating area.

•    Try updating your bedroom with a stylish telephone stand instead of a traditional nightstand.
