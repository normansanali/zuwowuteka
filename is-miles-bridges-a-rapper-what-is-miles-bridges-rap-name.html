Miles Bridges is an American professional basketball player for the Charlotte Hornets of the National Basketball Association.

He was born on March 21, 1998,(aged 24 years) in Flint, Michigan, the United States to his parents Raymond Bridges – two-time basketball state champion at Flint Northern High School, and Cynthia Bridges. 

He played college basketball for the Michigan State Spartans as a Power forward / Small forward and stands at a height of 2.01 m.

He is from a family of two siblings and has a beautiful sister named, Tara Rushing.
