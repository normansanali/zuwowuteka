Kylie Jenner Steps Out in Skintight Denim Dress and Matching Bag

 Posted on September 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kylie Jenner gave a luxe spin to the cowgirl aesthetic this week, when she stepped out in a skintight denim dress and knee-high boots.

The blue acid-wash midi dress was pulled straight from Y/Project's fall 2023 runway. It has a front zipper that runs through a shredded patchwork embellishment featuring the brand's name and the words Paris since 2013 and Best. The seams of the intricately crafted piece run along the sides, creating a flattering silhouette, and the long sleeves, also featuring zippers, flair at the hands.

Jenner carried a matching denim bag by the brand with the same patchwork and finished the look with a pair of brown vintage Gucci x Tom Ford stiletto boots, black-tinted Loewe sunglasses, and black oversized rose earrings.

Kylie Jenner/Instagram

She went full glam with her beauty look, flaunting a matte brown lip, rosy makeup, and a soft wavy hairdo. Her manicure was painted dark brown to match her boots.

Jenner shared photos on Instagram of herself in the fit, posing in front of her silver Mercedes-Benz G-Class in her home. "Out n about," she simply captioned the post.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder wore the look to support sister Kendall Jenner at her 818 Tequila investor's event in Beverly Hills. Kim Kardashian and Khloé Kardashian also attended the fete—the SKKN founder in black pantaboots and an asymmetrical bodysuit, and the Good American cofounder in a long-sleeved white minidress and matching over-the-knee boots.
